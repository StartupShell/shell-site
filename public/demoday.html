Demo Day

December 5th, 2014

What Is Demo Day?

At Demo Day, Startup Shell members will present their ventures, demo their products, and discuss their progress to fellow members, investors, friends and family.

This is a great opportunity for the community to get a peek as to what is going on inside the Shell. Awesome people are building awesome products and we want to share what we have been working on with the public.

Live Stream

Live stream will start at 4pm!
